# Blog Category Guidelines

## Category Overview

Each category has a distinct purpose and tone. Choose the appropriate approach based on the category specified in the blog topic.

---

## guides

**Purpose:** How-to articles, practical advice, decision-helper content, first-timer guidance

**Tone:** Helpful, practical, instructional

**Content Approach:**
- Step-by-step when appropriate
- Answer common questions directly
- Anticipate reader concerns
- Provide clear next actions
- Include booking/planning tips

**Example Topics:**
- "How to Book Your First Lake District Spa Day"
- "What to Bring to a Lake District Spa"
- "Spa Etiquette: What You Need to Know"
- "How to Choose Between a Spa Hotel and Day Spa"

**Key Elements:**
- Clear explanations of processes
- Practical checklists or considerations
- Common mistakes to avoid
- What to expect

---

## comparisons

**Purpose:** Side-by-side spa comparisons, feature comparisons, helping readers choose between options

**Tone:** Balanced but opinionated - provide clear recommendations with reasoning

**Content Approach:**
- Present options fairly
- Highlight key differences
- Give specific recommendations
- Use data/specifics to support comparisons
- Include "best for" scenarios

**Example Topics:**
- "Outdoor Hot Tubs vs Indoor Pools: Which Lake District Spa is Right for You?"
- "Windermere vs Keswick: Where Should You Book Your Spa Break?"
- "Day Spa vs Spa Hotel: Which Offers Better Value?"

**Key Elements:**
- Clear comparison points
- Specific spa examples
- Price/value considerations
- Direct recommendations with reasons

---

## seasonal

**Purpose:** Occasion-specific content (holidays, seasons, celebrations)

**Tone:** Occasion-focused, gift-oriented, timely

**Content Approach:**
- Tie to specific occasion/season
- Include gift-giving angle when relevant
- Suggest appropriate spa experiences
- Consider timing and booking windows
- Mention seasonal spa features (outdoor tubs in winter, etc.)

**Example Topics:**
- "Best Lake District Spas for Mother's Day"
- "Valentine's Day Spa Breaks in the Lake District"
- "Autumn Spa Escapes: Where to Go in September-November"
- "Winter Wellness: Spas with Outdoor Hot Tubs"

**Key Elements:**
- Occasion-specific recommendations
- Booking timeline advice
- Seasonal considerations
- Gift package mentions

---

## facilities

**Purpose:** Educational content about spa features, demystifying spa terminology, explaining what different facilities offer

**Tone:** Informative, educational, accessible

**Content Approach:**
- Explain spa terminology clearly
- Describe what to expect from facilities
- Compare facility types
- Help readers understand what they're booking
- Remove intimidation factor

**Example Topics:**
- "What is a Thermal Suite? Lake District Spas Explained"
- "Hydrotherapy Pools vs Regular Pools: What's the Difference?"
- "Understanding Spa Treatment Types"
- "What to Expect from a Rasul Chamber"

**Key Elements:**
- Clear definitions
- What the experience is like
- Which spas have specific facilities
- Who would enjoy this facility

---

## locations

**Purpose:** Area-specific guides, "spas near X" content, location-based recommendations

**Tone:** Local expertise, practical logistics, area knowledge

**Content Approach:**
- Focus on specific geographic area
- Include driving times and access info
- Mention nearby attractions/villages
- Give area-specific recommendations
- Consider accessibility from different starting points

**Example Topics:**
- "Best Spas in Windermere: Where to Book"
- "Keswick Spa Guide: Top Options in the Northern Lakes"
- "Spas Near Ambleside: Complete Guide"
- "Southern Lake District Spas: Grange, Cartmel, and Beyond"

**Key Elements:**
- Geographic specificity
- Driving times/distances
- Parking information
- Nearby amenities
- Local context

---

## Category Selection Guide

When assigned a category, adjust your approach accordingly:

- **guides** → Focus on "how" and practical steps
- **comparisons** → Present options with clear recommendations
- **seasonal** → Tie to timing and occasions
- **facilities** → Educate and demystify
- **locations** → Emphasize geography and access
