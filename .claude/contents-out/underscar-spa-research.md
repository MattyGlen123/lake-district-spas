# The Spa at Underscar — Research Summary

## Property Overview
- **Type**: Luxury self-catering holiday cottage resort (NOT a hotel) — 25 cottages
- **Accommodation**: 5-star 1, 2, and 3-bedroom cottages (converted 19th-century coach house and walled garden)
- **Location**: Applethwaite, Keswick, Cumbria, CA12 4PH
- **Position**: On the sheltered slopes of Skiddaw, looking out over Keswick and Derwentwater
- **Phone**: 017687 71500
- **Email**: salesandrentals@underscar.co.uk
- **Website**: https://www.underscar.co.uk/spa/
- **Booking system**: https://underscar.try.be/

## Spa Facilities
- **13-metre indoor heated swimming pool**
- **Hydrothermal jacuzzi** (with massage jets)
- **Eucalyptus-infused sauna**
- **Steam room**
- **Experience shower** (mentioned on SpaSeekers)
- **Relaxation room**
- **Gym** (Technogym equipped)
- **Treatment rooms** (soft lighting, calming music)
- **Bistro** (drinks and afternoon tea)

## Access Policies
- **Cottage guests**: Complimentary access to pool, jacuzzi, sauna, steam room, and gym included with all stays
- **Day visitors**: Welcome — spa day packages available (half-day from £80, full-day from £150)
- **Age restriction**: Minimum age 16 for treatments, spa packages and spa access. Younger children may stay in cottages and enjoy designated family swim times. Adults-only late at night and early in the morning.
- **Spa memberships**: Available (details not fully published online)
