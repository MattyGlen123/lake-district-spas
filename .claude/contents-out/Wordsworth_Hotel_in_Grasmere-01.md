# Wordsworth Hotel Grasmere — Research Summary

## Ownership & Rebranding

- Acquired by **The Inn Collection Group** in 2022
- Being rebranded as **"The Wordsworth Inn"**
- Inn Collection Group operates pub/inn style properties across northern England and Wales
- Their nearby property **The Swan in Grasmere** is open and taking redirected bookings

## Renovation Timeline

- **Closed:** 14 October 2024
- **Reopening:** Q1 2026 at the earliest, pending planning approval
- Expedia listing showed closure dates 6 June 2025 to 31 January 2026 (likely outdated, hotel closed earlier in Oct 2024)
- Multi-million-pound investment

## What's Changing

### Removed
- **Pool and spa facilities permanently removed** — replaced with additional bedrooms

### Added/Updated
- Room count increasing from 38 to 54 bedrooms (7 dog friendly)
- New state of the art kitchen
- Restaurant and lounges redesigned for relaxed dining
- 196 indoor covers and 148 outdoor covers
- New lift and two fully accessible rooms
- Helicopter landing pad maintained for Air Ambulance use

## Key Takeaway for FAQ

The answer to "Does the Wordsworth Hotel in Grasmere have a spa?" is **no, not anymore**. The spa and pool have been permanently removed during the renovation. The hotel is reopening as an inn focused on dining and accommodation, not wellness.

This is significant because "wordsworth spa grasmere" gets 5,000 monthly searches with Low competition — many people will be searching for a spa that no longer exists. Strong opportunity to capture that traffic and redirect to the two Grasmere spas we do list (Daffodil Hotel Spa and Rothay Garden by Harbour Hotels).
